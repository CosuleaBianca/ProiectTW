Harry Potter

From the cupboard under the stairs to a whole new life at Hogwarts, Harry Potter’s journey has as many ups and downs as a Gryffindor vs Slytherin Quidditch match. When he starts at Hogwarts, Harry’s main worry is that he won’t be as good at magic as the children from wizarding families. If only he knew! Through time, Harry realises just how important he really is. And through it all, Harry is always a true Gryffindor: brave, true, and loyal. And he always values friendship and love above everything.
